A Introduction of My PhD Research

My research focuses on making sense of big data with effective and agile data analytics, especially with hashing techniques. In particular, I worked on graph stream classification and hashing technique innovation.

Currently, my work focuses on cognitive data analytics, in particular on big data analysis in social media and impact analysis between events and time series.
